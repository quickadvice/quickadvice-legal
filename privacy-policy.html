Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

QuickAdvice ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the QuickAdvice mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Device Identifier: We automatically generate and store an anonymous device identifier (UUID) on your device when you first open the app. This identifier is used solely to track your free question usage and subscription status. It is not linked to your name, email address, or any other personally identifiable information.

Questions and Answers (Keep My History mode only): If you choose "Keep My History" mode, the questions you ask and the answers you receive are stored in our secure database. This allows you to refer back to past guidance at any time.

Anonymous Mode: If you choose "Remain Anonymous" mode, the content of your questions and answers is never stored. Only non-identifying metadata is recorded (expert category selected, timestamp, and whether the question was free or paid) for operational purposes.

Usage Data: We track how many free questions you have used in the current month to enforce the free tier limit. This data is tied to your device identifier only.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide the QuickAdvice service and generate AI responses
  • Track free question usage and enforce usage limits
  • Manage subscription status
  • Improve the quality of our service
  • Respond to support requests

3. AI-Generated Responses

Your questions are transmitted to Anthropic's Claude AI model to generate responses. By using QuickAdvice, you agree that your question content may be processed by Anthropic's API. Anthropic's privacy policy governs their handling of this data. We do not share your questions with any other third parties.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a SOC 2 compliant database provider. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or destruction.

5. Data Retention

If you use Keep My History mode, your questions and answers are retained until you delete them via Settings → Clear My History, or until you delete your app data. In Anonymous Mode, no question content is ever stored.

6. In-App Purchases

All payments are processed through Google Play Billing or Apple In-App Purchase. We do not collect or store your payment card information. Subscription management is handled entirely by Google Play or the Apple App Store.

7. Children's Privacy

QuickAdvice is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
